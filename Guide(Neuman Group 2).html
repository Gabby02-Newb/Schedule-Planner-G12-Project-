How to Use the Schedule Planner

Step 1: Create Your Schedule

  • Start by adding tasks or events to your planner.
  • Use color codes for different categories of tasks (e.g., study, meetings, personal).
  • Set reminders to ensure you don't miss important tasks.

Step 2: Prioritize Your Tasks

  • Use prioritization methods like the Eisenhower Matrix to organize tasks by urgency and importance.
  • Mark your high-priority tasks so you focus on them first.

Step 3: View and Adjust

  • Switch between daily, weekly, and monthly views to get a better understanding of your schedule.
  • Adjust tasks if your schedule changes, and ensure you're always on track.
