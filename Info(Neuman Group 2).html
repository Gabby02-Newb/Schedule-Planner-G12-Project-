WHAT IS THE TOPIC ABOUT

  • The topic focuses on the effectiveness of a web-based schedule planner for student time management.
  • A schedule planner organizes and allocates time for specific tasks or subjects.
  • Examples: Google Calendar, Notion, Trello, MyStudyLife.
  • Features include reminders, color coding, time blocking, and integration tools.
  • Helps improve prioritization, routine, and productivity.

MAIN TOPIC

  • Definition: A system used to organize time and manage tasks.
  • Purpose: Improve time management, productivity, reduce stress, and track goals.
  • Types: Manual planners, digital apps, academic planners, work planners.
  • Key Features: Time blocking, reminders, sync, automatic updates.
  • Benefits: Routine building, focus enhancement, long-term planning, work-life balance.

KEY FOCUS

  • Online accessibility for task management.
  • Prioritization methods: Eisenhower Matrix, ABC analysis.
  • Multiple views: daily, weekly, monthly.
  • Integration with other productivity tools.
  • User-friendly and customizable interface.

CORE OBJECTIVE

  • Measure whether the planner improves student time management.
  • Reduce procrastination and increase productivity.
  • Promote goal-oriented planning and decision-making.
  • Support balance between academic and personal life.
  • Provide visual scheduling and stress reduction tools.
